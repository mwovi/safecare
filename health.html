Best Of Health care for you

Our company as it currently exist as a model of health care delivery that combines home based care for medical needs, management and care co-ordination.

We provide care that is safe, compassionate, ethical, patient and family centered as well as evidence based to enhance quality of life, accessibility, continuity of care and improve patient outcome.

We aim to be outstanding care providers within the country providing our clients with quality, safe, first class care, using every means available to achieve the best possible outcome for them. To deliver high quality care to improve and maintain a healthy quality of life for all people while using a holistic person- centred care approach in the delivery of medical and non medical home care and social services support.

We believe in creating safe and secure environment for our clients who are vulnerable and have complex needs. We understand their needs and respond to them sensitively while creating sense of security and comfort.we are able to understand our clients emotional and mental well-being hence providing more personalized care and tailor our support to meet their individual needs.

We value each of our clients as an individual, respect aspirations and their commitment. We seek to understand and promote priorities, needs, abilities and limits when providing care we treat our clients with dignity, we aim to support and empower them to respect their right to make their own decisions.

We are open about our services. We provide our clients with the services that we have undertaken to provide and we do not take a client where we do not have the capability to provide the services needed. We charge clients in line with what we have recorded in our contract.

We take into account the views of all those involved in the care of clients as they are valuable to be part of the team, we listen to their contributions and input.

As SafeCare it is our commitment to improve the quality of life of our clients by providing safe, high quality services that exceed industries standards. We believe we can provide the best possible care of we have trained and retained the best staffs who share our values and committed to our vision.
